Impact of AI on Employment & Productivity

Overview
This project analyzes how Artificial Intelligence (AI) adoption impacts employment trends, salary growth, and productivity across industries.
The goal is to identify whether increasing AI adoption leads to job displacement, salary shifts, or productivity gains.

Objectives
- Analyze employment trends before and after AI adoption
- Measure salary changes across different roles
- Evaluate productivity improvements linked to AI
- Identify industries most affected by AI

Dataset
- Source: [Impact of AI on employment and productivity dataset]
- Data includes:
  - Job roles
  - AI adoption level
  - Salary before AI
  - Salary after AI
  - Productivity metrics
  - Industry classification

 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Python (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Excel (Initial Data Handling)

Key Insights
- Higher AI adoption correlates with increased productivity
- Certain roles show salary growth, especially tech-related jobs
- Some repetitive roles show decline or slower growth
- AI adoption impact varies significantly by industry

Dashboard Features
- AI Adoption vs Salary Change %
- Employment Trends Analysis
- Productivity Comparison (Before vs After AI)
- Industry-wise Impact Breakdown
